Diamond in the Rough is the world’s leading natural, rough diamond jewelry brand. Their jewelry showcases the inherent beauty of the gemstones in their untouched form.

The Objective

Simande’s role in this project was to effectively bring an in-person retail experience to the web.

The Execution

We created customer engagement through a “Favorites” feature that allows users to identify a product that they liked but might not be ready to purchase. We also developed an “abandoned cart” feature that notifieds users to return to the website if they have unpurchased products in their shopping cart.

The Final Product

What Simande created was a unique and engaging retail experience exclusive to this luxury rough diamond jewelry brand.

Onepager is the easiest way to build a great web presence for your small business. Quickly build a site that looks great on mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers.

The Objective

The team at Simande wanted to empower small business owners with the ability to easily and quickly get their business online.

The Execution

We envisioned and developed a product that was quick to learn and easy to use so even the most novice computer users are able to build a website on their own.

The application includes many drag and drop features that allow the user to add new content to their site. The user can choose a pre-designed theme or customize one that is unique to their brand.

The Dashboard

For many business owners, getting to know their customer base is important. We built a simple dashboard embedded with complex functionality. It allows the user to track their website performance, send out newsletters to their customers, and manage other advanced features.

The Final Product

Onepager is an application packed with many sophisticated features coupled with an elegant interface that makes it easy to use.

Website: onepagerapp.com
